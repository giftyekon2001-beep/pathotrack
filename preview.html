Health Education Hub

Learn about infection prevention and public health

Hygiene

Hand Hygiene

Wash your hands frequently with soap and clean water for at least 20 seconds, especially before eating and after using the toilet.

Hygiene

Respiratory Hygiene

Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue when you cough or sneeze. Dispose of tissues properly and wash hands afterward.

Food Safety

Safe Food Handling

Cook food to safe temperatures, keep raw and cooked foods separate, refrigerate promptly, and wash fruits and vegetables before eating.

Water Safety

Clean Drinking Water

Drink safe, clean water. Boil water if unsure about its safety. Use water purification methods when needed. Avoid untreated water sources.

Prevention

Vaccination

Keep up with recommended vaccinations. Vaccinations protect you and those around you from serious diseases. Consult your healthcare provider.

Prevention

Social Distancing

Maintain physical distance from sick people. During disease outbreaks, follow local health guidelines on distancing and quarantine measures.
